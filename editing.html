Revising and Editing for Clarity

Once you’ve drafted a paper, you can use AI to identify awkward phrasing, improve clarity, and even check grammar. You can prompt: “Make this more concise” or “Rewrite in formal academic tone.”

Academic Integrity Reminder: Always maintain ownership of your ideas. AI may polish your language, but if large portions of text are rewritten without acknowledgment, this can raise integrity concerns. Check your institution’s policies on acceptable writing assistance.
