Summarizing Articles and Clarifying Complex Language

If you struggle with academic language or long articles, AI tools can help summarize content, define jargon, and identify key arguments. You might ask: “Summarize this in plain language” or “What is the author’s main argument?”

Academic Integrity Reminder: Use summaries as a comprehension aid—not as a substitute for reading. Avoid using AI-generated summaries as citations unless clearly indicated and allowed by your instructor.
